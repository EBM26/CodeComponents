Leo (Jul 22 - Aug 23)

Sign Overview

Leo is the Fixed stage of the Fire Element and is the ruled by the Sun. This means that they gather and stabilize (Fixed stage) the Sun's energies of warmth and love in order to bestow them on to others so that they may act and live (Fire Element), exactly like the Sun gives its energy to the rest of the planets and stars. Just as the Sun is the center of the universe surrounded by many planets and stars, so too does the Leo believe they are the center of the world and are constantly surrounded by others that agree with this belief.

The Fixed stage is what makes Leo confident and capable of achieving their goals. It makes them great organizers, managers, and leaders of others. Like all the other Fixed signs, it also makes Leo stable, stubborn, and reliable. The Fire Element is what makes them love action, be spontaneous, passionate, outgoing, enthusiastic, visionary, and instinctual.

Symbol Meaning

Leo's symbol is a lion. This is expressed by them being the king of others like the lion is the king of the jungle animals. This also means that they are sensual, highly protective of their surroundings/loved ones/possessions, brave, proud, often lazy in the males, and often very productive in the females. The Leo roars often without serious intent of harm, they just want their pain to be heard. However, their rage and anger are not to be tested.

Karmic Stage

Leo represents the "Teenager" or "Adolescent" stage in the Astrological Karmic Cycle. Right after the "Pre-Teen" stage of Cancer that is sensitive, lacks the self-confidence, and needs affirmations of worth, the Leo is full of confidence in their abilities and self-worth. Not only do they believe that they are the right person for the job, they believe they are the ONLY right person for the job. Having passed the younger stages of the Karmic Cycle, Leo are sensitive to others' feelings since they experienced emotional vulnerabilities in those earlier stages.

Not only do Leo believe that they are the right person for the job, they believe they are the ONLY right person for the job.

Just like a teenager, Leo lacks the respect of their elders, dismissing their advice, and doing things the way they feel is right. Since Leo are born in the summer days, their souls are mesmerized by the hot Sun and want to enjoy life as much as possible. Just like a teenager that first discovers the pleasures of relating with the opposite sex, Leo are obsessed with romance and love and sometimes rush into relationships that aren't healthy for them. Their main mantra in life is "I want". Their goals in this karmic stage are to teach that "love is enthusiasm" and to learn that "love is humility".

How to Spot an Leo

Leo have a medium to tall height, seldom short. They are broad across the shoulders and narrow hipped with an athletic built. Their face features are bold and handsome. Their foreheads are broad and flat with the eyes looking out from under it. They usually has a thick head of hair that waves backwards, just like a lion's mane.

Leo's most distinguished feature is the proud manner in which they carry themselves, standing as tall as they can, chest out, and head held up high as is fit for a king/queen. Leo move and speak in a calm, slow manner. A king rushes for no one. They are easy to spot within a group, usually the center of focus and attention; always involved. Their kindness and warmth are very noticeable when one comes in contact with Leo. They are the most friendly and inviting sign of all.

Strengths vs. Weaknesses

Leo's strong traits are: warm-heartedness, kindness, generosity, charisma, leadership, courage, and gentleness. These strengths can potentially turn into weaknesses and become: arrogance, foolish pride, tyranny, boastfulness, and over-controlling. Leo's charisma and natural ability to become the center of attention is unmatched. They do not give up easily and are certain they will reach their goal or beat their adversaries.

Leo's favorite weakness is playing the crowd; a group of people cheering on Leo can get them to do just about anything.

Leo are suckers for people who compliment and appreciate their abilities and thus can sometimes be fooled or corrupted by slick talkers with malicious intentions. They posses a great fashion sense and look good in anything they wear because of their high self-worth and self-confidence. Leo are loved by all and are able to get away with offenses others cannot. They have a weakness for gambling and business ventures that get them into financial problems if they are unsuccessful. They also have a weakness for over-spending and purchasing. Leo's favorite weakness is playing the crowd; a group of people cheering on Leo can get them to do just about anything.

Good Paths in Life vs. Pitfalls

Leo have a built in confidence that they are strong and highly capable people. At the same time, they are also afraid of taking upon themselves challenges that they might fail in. Leo have a need to be successful and victorious in all they do, lest their fragile pride be hurt. This attitude can keep them from trying new challenges and leaving the comfort zone they so strongly cling to.

Leo's pride and love of being praised can also render them vulnerable to insults and embarrassments which might hurt them greatly. This same pride and love of praise also renders them vulnerable to slick-talking, knifing people who might use this weak spot into manipulating the generous Leo into doing their biddings.

Their pride can potentially turn into arrogance and a feeling that they are all-knowing and omnipotent. This can cause Leo to not take warning or advice from anyone and potentially take a mighty fall. Leo naturally believes they are king and in charge of others that are "beneath" them and sometimes become bossy and controlling of others.

All of these issues can be remedied if Leo strive to achieve their karmic lesson: humility. If Leo add just enough humility to their lives (not too much, they are still kings/queens), they will not be such a prisoner of their destructive ego.

Likes / Hobbies

Since Leo are a Fire element, they enjoy being active and being around people. They usually enjoy sports and will often prefer to watch rather than compete when they find out they are not so good at it and can't guarantee a victories. The theater and drama are one of the Leo's favorite past-times and it is no surprise that many famous actors throughout the history were/are Leos. Acting provides an opportunity for the Leo to showcase their most precious and prideful possession; themselves!

Leo don't do things just for the fun; they make sure they excel in everything they venture in. They also love the arts and crafts. Whatever Leo will do, they will make sure that their friends and admirers are around them to compliment and cheer them on. Leo love playing the crowd. They have a royal taste in all that they choose; their clothes, food, cars, etc. will usually be of exquisite quality.

Dislikes / Pet Peeves

Leo hate being talked to crudely, insultingly, disrespectfully or any other way that do not suite their royal manner. They hate failing or losing. They are very protective of their position, loved ones, possessions, or anything else that has to do with them and hate when people are disrespectful to any of the above mentioned.

Leo strongly dislike when people undermine their authority or don't listen to what they say. They dislike pettiness or any other behavior unfit for people of their royal stature. Leo are sensitive to others' feelings and cannot stand to see other people's feelings hurt, even if they do not belong to their circle.

How to Approach a Leo

The most important concept to grasp when approaching a Leo is that we are approaching people who believe they are royalty. Therefore, we must be cautious in the way we speak and behave towards Leo. If we treat them with respect and give an occasional shower of praise and compliments, we will find that our new friends will return ten folds in warmth, favors, and love.

The other part of this equation is that we need to be aware that just like a king, Leo believe they have a given right to rule others, tell them what to do, dispense their critique/advice without being asked, and decide for others what they want. Once we have accepted that, we can be assured that Leo will take care of us better than they takes care of themselves. We can also get what we want from them by asking softly, acting helpless, or sweet-talking them with praises and compliments.

Save your criticisms for a different sign, Leo despise being criticized and would quicker get a new friend than admit they did something wrong.

We should also be aware that Leo tend to be over-dramatic at times when they feel that people are disrespecting them and that we should just lend a listening ear and try to gently soothe their pains. Remember, Leo roar a lot and loudly but that does not necessarily mean they are about to bite, they just want to be heard by all. Relationship Aspirations

Leo have the warmest heart and the most charisma of the entire Zodiac and therefore are quick to fall in love with a mate they think is to their liking. Leo love beauty and so that is important in a mate. Since they believes themselves to be of royal blood, a mate of a classier, more refined manner is required. Leo are not ones to be rude or ill-mannered and neither do they like to be around people that are. Leo want someone they can give their over-flowing love, warmth, and generosity who will in turn admire them and shower them with compliments.

They want someone who will not mind them taking charge and giving out orders, someone who will march to the beat of their drum. Leo is a jealous mate, both males and females, yet they themselves like to flirt and be admired be the opposite sex. Rest assured Leo are very loyal mates. The flirting is just an extension of their love of admiration. Leo is a big spender, both males and females, and needs a mate that will accept that or at least not make a big deal of it. Leo like the finer things in life and that is a fact that will never change.
