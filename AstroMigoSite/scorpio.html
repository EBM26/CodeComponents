Scorpio (Oct 23 - Nov 22)

Sign Overview

Scorpio is the Fixed stage of the Water element and is ruled by the solar side of Mars. This means that Scorpio uses Mars' pure and powerful energy to monitor and secure (Fixed stage) emotions and behaviors (Water element). It is the strong, pure, and concentrated energy of Mars passing through the Water element of Scorpio that makes them so passionate, highly-motivated, competitive, physically strong, and highly energetic. The Fixed stage is what makes Scorpio stable, reliable, in control, and stubborn. The Water element is what makes them intuitive, nurturing, compassionate, imaginative, and sensitive.

Symbol Meaning

Scorpio's symbol is a scorpion, of course. This is expressed by Scorpio appearing to be small and harmless but in reality packing a dangerous threat with their stinging tale and stealth abilities. Scorpio are also very defensive and will make sure to sting anyone who crosses their comfort zone. There is another symbol to Scorpio that not many are aware about; the eagle. The eagle symbol is represented by Scorpio's ability to rise above human boundaries and achieve their goals just like an eagle rises above the land. It also represents Scorpio's ability to renew themselves (using the regeneration powers of Scorpio's other planetary ruler, Pluto) after a big fall just like the Phoenix bird renews itself from the ashes.

Karmic Stage

Scorpio represents the "Sexuality" stage in the Astrological Karmic Cycle. After the soul has finished with the stage of Libra, in which it was busy starting new relationships and expressing fair judgments, the soul has moved on to a different aspect; personal empowerment. The soul in the stage of Scorpio becomes infatuated with every aspect of the "self", from birth to death and everything in between. This powerful passion that Scorpio buries under many layers of self-restraint is what propels them to go out and explore every feeling and sensation in the world. They must reach the very depths of truth and conquer all their fears in order to achieve their main life goal; ultimate power.

How to Spot an Scorpio

Scorpio usually have a medium or short height; seldom tall. Their overall build is broad framed. The weight seems to be concentrated on the lower half of their body. Their legs are often short, giving the impression that Scorpio is shorter than they actually are. Scorpio's most distinguished feature is the concentrated, electrifying power which surrounds their presence. Their other most distinguished feature is their penetrating, hypnotizing, and powerful gaze. It's as if Scorpio's eyes are penetrating deep into your soul and reading your every secret.

Their eyebrows are set in a little frown. Scorpio's movements are very powerful and restrained; never moving more than necessary. At a social scene, Scorpio can be spotted standing on the side, gazing at their next prey or engaging in a passionate conversation with someone of interest while their penetrating eyes are staring straight into their partner's eyes. Rarely will you come across a Scorpio whose eyes shift constantly.

Good Paths in Life vs. Pitfalls

Scorpio are very physically and mentally powerful people who are equipped with very strong wills and thus few things ever stand in their way. However, their biggest enemy can be their own selves. Like the rest of the Water signs, Scorpio tend to carry the emotional weight of the world on their shoulders. Furthermore, they have great difficulty dealing with lack of success and letting go or forgiving any physical or emotional damages done to them by others.

All of these combinations sometimes lead Scorpio to a periodic melancholy and anger which they tend to suppress by abusing substances or committing any other self-destructive behaviors. Like a scorpion surrounded by smoke who stings themselves to death, Scorpio tend to self-destruct with bad habits, suppressed anger, and hurt. A good advice for the Scorpio is to learn to forgive and turn to their eagle powers of renewal that enable them to rise from the deepest ashes. Interestingly enough, it is also Scorpio's violent, sadistic manner paired up with their fascination for life, death, and mysticism that they can channel into becoming great physicians or surgeons.

Another important potential pitfall for the Scorpio is their need to always control everything and manipulate everyone. This need stems from a constant paranoia that if they lose control, they will be in danger or lose their power. This constant need for total control can lead Scorpio to neurosis and alienate their loved ones who do not wish to be controlled. A good advice would be for them to meditate, relax, and trust in the workings of a higher power they believe in to protect them and correctly manage their life.

Scorpio are very sensual and self-aware of the tastes, colors, smells, and textures that they engage in. Therefore, they have a very rich and luxurious taste when it comes to food, clothes, perfumes, etc. Scorpio like their food bold and well spiced; they are known to be lovers of spicy food. Scorpio's clothes are usually fine in texture and high in quality. They prefer the color black which expresses restrained strength, just like they do. Red is another favorite color of theirs due to the influence of Mars.

Pastimes aren't a Scorpio's forte. The very name suggests something Scorpio are against; passing the time. Their lives are way too busy and fast-paced to just pass the time away, preferring to spend their time working or pursuing a passion of their. If Scorpio do play games or sports, they would be ones that require intense concentration or are risky and dangerous; Scorpio are quite the daredevils who enjoy conquering their fears. Deep sea diving is a sport Scorpio enjoys greatly. Scorpio represent the "Sexuality" stage which means, of course, that human sexuality is of great interest and pleasure to them.

How to Approach a Scorpio

Scorpio is very suspicious of others and highly protective of themselves. Therefore, you must be careful approaching a Scorpio with anything but friendly intentions. Even then, Scorpio will be quite suspicious of anyone approaching them out of the blue; remember, they don't really like surprises. Although Scorpio's defenses and suspicions will be raised before you approach, you will not notice any behavior that gives away their feelings. On the contrary, Scorpio, masters of self-control, might even show you bright and smiling faces as they warmly shake your hand and talk to you. Make no mistake; they are examining your every move, gesture, word, tone of voice, body language, etc. Scorpio will not rest until they precisely calibrate the person standing in front of them.

Even after you have managed to pass Scorpio's initial exams, you will still be "under surveillance" for a very long time before they will reward you with their greatest gift; their trust. Scorpio are extremely selective in who they become friends with because to them, true friendships are meant to be intimate and last a long time. Before going into any type of relationship, Scorpio ask themselves "how much will I have to invest in this relationship?" and "will I be giving more than I am receiving?".

In Scorpio, you will find a kind, loving, compassionate, loyal, and fun friend. A friend who will be at your side during your toughest moments till the very end. A friend who is willing to give their life in order to protect yours. Such are Scorpio, passionate in every aspect of their lives. Scorpio are people of their word; they mean what they say and do what they promise. Beware, they can also be very straight-forward with the people close to them, sparing no judgment or harsh words if they see fit. From your Scorpio friend you will always get a brutally honest opinion. The same is true in regards to compliments. They do not give many, but the ones they do give are honest, precise, and meaningful.

As you can see, Scorpio couldn't care less what others think or say about them. However, you should be careful never to insult a Scorpio, they are quite sensitive. If you did manage to pierce their tough exoskeleton, they will rarely tell you. Instead, they will withdraw their friendship from you or even worse, exact revenge if they see fit. Scorpio find it difficult and dangerous to share their most personal emotions; there are secrets in their lives they will never share with anyone on this earth. If you attempt to dig up any of these hidden treasures, Scorpio will nonchalantly lie to you about it or avoid the subject in some way. Pressuring them to tell you would be your last mistake in their eyes.

Lying to a Scorpio is futile; they are a human lie detector. Although Scorpio are fun to hang out with, you should know that they are highly competitive, even in regards to a simple game. Scorpio was born to win and they will do so even if it kills them... or you. You should also know that Scorpio are manipulative and possessive of their friends, usually wanting control of the situation, mood, tempo, etc. Their passion knows no boundaries and if you are truly a Scorpio's friend, you will spend many hours listening to them furiously describing the wrong doings of others in this matter or that. Scorpio need your listening ear to comfort their raging soul and express their anger in a safe environment.
