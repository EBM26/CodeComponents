Chart of Alkaline and Acidic Foods

The information on this chart was taken from the following PDF file https://liveenergized.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/alkaline-food-chart-4.0.pdf and reflects their studies and finding.

Please note that one * marks that the food item is low in alkaline or acidity, two ** mark that the food item is medium in alkaline or acidity, and three *** mark that the food item is high in alkaline or acidity.
